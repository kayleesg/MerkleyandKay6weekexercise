  • Strength:

    3 x 20 Dumbbell Rows - As Heavy As Possible

    Can you even recover, bro?:

    1:00 min rest between sets

    Score is fastest time

    Static Stretches


Day 2 Week 1

  • Warm up:

    Sound the Alarm

    20 air squats

    15 inch worms with reach

    10 russian KB swings

    5 KB windmills (each arm)

  • Strength:

    Make sure to warm-up properly with some lighter reps to avoid injury, this is just the first week afterall.

    Test: Bench Press - 3 Rep Max (the reason for 3 rep max is that you may not have a spotter, and going to 1 RM increases the risk of injury.)

    Make sure to work up to your 3 RM. You have 5-minutes to reach your 3 RM.

    Once done do a Weighted Plank with a 15 lb plate 3 times, 1 minute each time

  • WOD:

    Seeing Triple:

    4 min As-many-reps-as-possible (AMRAP)

    7 reps of each exercise:

      Sit ups
      Step ups on a 16" box
      V ups
      Box jumps on a 16" box
      Toes to bar
      Burpee box jumps

  • Cool down:

    15 minute slow row on the row machine

    Slow arm wind mills

    Static stretches
